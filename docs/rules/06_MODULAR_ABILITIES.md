# Modular Abilities & Keywords

*Draft - Subject to change.*

To further customize units and abstract complex concepts (like magic, wings, or heavy weaponry) without adding bloated rules, you can purchase Modular Abilities (Keywords) for your units. 

When creating a unit, add or subtract the corresponding point value to/from the unit's total cost.

## Positive Keywords (Increase Cost)

These abilities provide tactical advantages on the battlefield.

- **Flying (+10 pts):** This model ignores the AP penalty for moving into Difficult Terrain. It costs them the standard 2 AP to move into any Zone, regardless of terrain.
- **Blast (+15 pts):** When this model declares a Ranged Attack against a Zone, it generates 1 automatic hit against every other enemy model in that same Zone (before Dodge is applied). 
- **Sniper (+10 pts):** This model ignores the +1 Dodge bonus provided by Light Cover when shooting. 
- **Teleport (+15 pts):** Once per game, this model may spend 3 AP to instantly move to any Zone within Line of Sight, bypassing the normal adjacency rules for movement.
- **Aura of Dread (+15 pts):** Enemy models within the same Zone as this model require +1 AP to execute a Fall Back action.

## Negative Keywords (Refund Points)

These abilities represent physical flaws, cumbersome equipment, or poor training. Adding them to a unit reduces its total point cost. A unit's point cost cannot drop below the base 5 points.

- **Slow (-10 pts):** This model may never move more than 1 Zone per activation, regardless of how much AP it has.
- **Clumsy / Frail (-10 pts):** Whenever this model suffers hits from an attack, add +1 to the number of successful hits *before* Armor is applied. (e.g., If 3 hits get past Dodge, they take 4 hits against their Armor).
- **Inaccurate (-5 pts):** When this model rolls its CMB pool, natural 6s do not generate Successes (they only hit on 4s and 5s).
