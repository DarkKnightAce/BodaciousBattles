# Modular Abilities & Keywords

*Draft - Subject to change.*

To further customize units and abstract complex concepts (like magic, wings, or heavy weaponry) without adding bloated rules, you can purchase Modular Abilities (Keywords) for your units. 

When creating a unit, spend the corresponding **Perk Points** from your model's pool to buy positive keywords, or add negative keywords to grant additional Perk Points.

---

## Positive Keywords (Cost Perk Points)

These abilities provide tactical advantages on the battlefield.

### Ranged
- **Cost:** 10 Perk Points
- **Requirements:** None
- **Description:** This model can spend 2 AP to make a Ranged attack against an enemy in a Zone to which it has Line of Sight, provided the model is not Engaged in melee.
- **Represents:** (e.g., a rifle, energy blaster, or bows)

### Boss
- **Cost:** 30 Perk Points
- **Requirements:** None
- **Description:** This model contributes two activation dice to the draw bag instead of one, activating twice per round.
- **Represents:** (e.g., a giant monster, a faction leader, or a highly trained solo hero)

### Flying
- **Cost:** 10 Perk Points
- **Requirements:** None
- **Description:** This model ignores the AP penalty for moving into Difficult Terrain. It costs them the standard 2 AP to move into any Zone, regardless of terrain.
- **Represents:** (e.g., wings, jump-jets, hovering technology, or wall-running agility)

### Blast
- **Cost:** 15 Perk Points
- **Requirements:** **Ranged** keyword
- **Description:** When this model declares a Ranged Attack against a Zone, it generates 1 automatic hit against every other enemy model in that same Zone (before Dodge is applied).
- **Represents:** (e.g., rocket launchers, grenades, magic fireballs, or heavy mortar artillery)

### Sniper
- **Cost:** 10 Perk Points
- **Requirements:** **Ranged** keyword
- **Description:** This model ignores the +1 Dodge bonus provided by Light Cover when shooting.
- **Represents:** (e.g., precision optics, specialized sniper training, or homing ammunition)

### Teleport
- **Cost:** 15 Perk Points
- **Requirements:** None
- **Description:** Once per game, this model may spend 3 AP to instantly move to any Zone within Line of Sight, bypassing the normal adjacency rules for movement.
- **Represents:** (e.g., displacement fields, shadow-stepping magic, or high-speed dash thrusters)

### Aura of Dread
- **Cost:** 15 Perk Points
- **Requirements:** None
- **Description:** Enemy models within the same Zone as this model require +1 AP to execute a Fall Back action.
- **Represents:** (e.g., terrifying size, psychological warfare gas, or gravity manipulation fields)

### Lethal Strike
- **Cost:** 10 Perk Points
- **Requirements:** None
- **Description:** This model generates 1 **Critical Point (CP)** from successful hits that roll a natural 5, in addition to the natural 6s that all models count. This makes it easier to reach the 2 CP threshold needed to set an attack's minimum damage to 1.
- **Represents:** (e.g., energy blades, monomolecular claws, poison coatings, or precision weak-point strikes)

### Vanguard
- **Cost:** 10 Perk Points
- **Requirements:** None
- **Description:** Before the first round's activation dice are drawn, this model can perform a free 1-Zone move.
- **Represents:** (e.g., jump-thruster scouts, infiltration cloaks, forward bike scouts, or light hover vehicles)

### Fortified
- **Cost:** 10 Perk Points
- **Requirements:** None
- **Description:** If this model does not move during its activation, it gains +1 Armor against the next attack targeting it before its next activation.
- **Represents:** (e.g., portable energy barriers, deploying stabilizing braces, portable shields, or heavy sandbags)

### Group
- **Cost:** 15 Perk Points
- **Requirements:** **Attrition** keyword
- **Description:** During this model's activation, it may split into 2 or more smaller models placed on the battlefield. Divide its current Wounds among the new models (minimum 1 Wound per model). Each split model retains the **Attrition** keyword and calculates its CMB from its individual Wound count. The original model is removed; each new model activates independently in future rounds.
- **Represents:** (e.g., a horde dividing to surround the enemy, a command squad breaking into fire teams, or a swarm organism spawning sub-swarms)

### Medic
- **Cost:** 15 Perk Points
- **Requirements:** None
- **Description:** Once per activation, this model may spend 2 AP to heal a model in the same Zone, restoring 1 lost Wound. A model's Wounds cannot exceed its starting maximum.
- **Represents:** (e.g., nanite stim-injectors, medical drones, restorative magic, or tactical first-aid kits)

## Negative Keywords (Grant Bonus Perk Points)

These abilities represent physical flaws, cumbersome equipment, or poor training. Adding them to a unit grants extra Perk Points to spend elsewhere.

### Slow
- **Cost:** Grants +10 Perk Points
- **Requirements:** None
- **Description:** This model may never move more than 1 Zone per activation, regardless of how much AP it has.
- **Represents:** (e.g., heavy power armor, short stubby legs, massive encumbrance, or a sluggish biological pace)

### Clumsy / Frail
- **Cost:** Grants +10 Perk Points
- **Requirements:** None
- **Description:** Whenever this model suffers hits from an attack, add +1 to the number of successful hits *before* Armor is applied.
- **Represents:** (e.g., thin armor plating, brittle bones, hollow-point vulnerability, or delicate inner circuitry)

### Attrition
- **Cost:** Grants +15 Perk Points
- **Requirements:** None
- **Description:** This model defines 3 CMB values corresponding to its remaining Wounds. When resolving attacks, use the CMB matching its current Wound threshold:
  - **Top third** (Wounds > 2/3 of max): peak CMB
  - **Middle third** (Wounds > 1/3 of max): reduced CMB
  - **Bottom third** (Wounds ≤ 1/3 of max): lowest CMB
- **Represents:** (e.g., a swarm whose members are picked off one by one, a battle tank whose weapons systems fail as it takes damage, or a raging beast that weakens as it bleeds)

### Inaccurate
- **Cost:** Grants +5 Perk Points
- **Requirements:** None
- **Description:** When this model rolls its CMB pool, natural 6s do not generate Successes (they only hit on 4s and 5s).
- **Represents:** (e.g., poor vision, wild firing posture, low-quality weaponry, or untamed combat rage)

### Short Range
- **Cost:** Grants +5 Perk Points
- **Requirements:** **Ranged** keyword
- **Description:** This model's ranged attacks can only target adjacent zones (within 1 zone distance).
- **Represents:** (e.g., flamethrowers, combat shotguns, throwing axes, or high-spread plasma jets)

### Combustible
- **Cost:** Grants +10 Perk Points
- **Requirements:** None
- **Description:** Whenever this model suffers damage from a Blast attack or enters a Hazardous Zone, it suffers 1 additional Wound.
- **Represents:** (e.g., volatile fuel cells, ammo backpack stockpiles, dry flammable straw, or unstable energy batteries)
