# Turn Structure

*Draft - Subject to change.*

## Game Rounds
A game of Bodacious Battles is played over a series of **Rounds**. During each Round, players will take turns activating their units.

## The Bag Pull Activation System
Bodacious Battles uses a randomized alternating activation system to create tension and simulate the chaos of the battlefield.

At the start of the **Action Phase**, players gather one D6 (matching their chosen player color) for *every* unit in their army that can activate. All of these dice are placed into a shared, opaque bag or cup.

1. **Draw:** A player blindly draws one D6 from the bag.
2. **Activate:** The player whose color was drawn chooses one of their eligible units to activate.
3. **Execute:** The chosen unit receives its Action Points (tracked via a D6 placed next to the model) and performs its actions.
4. **Repeat:** Once the unit's activation concludes, another die is drawn from the bag.

This continues until the bag is empty, at which point the Action Phase ends.

## Phases of a Round
1. **Strategy Phase:** Players generate Command Points (CP) based on their force's chosen Generation Mechanic, resolve any ongoing effects, and place their activation dice into the bag.
2. **Action Phase:** Players draw dice from the bag and activate units one by one.
3. **Cleanup Phase:** Remove AP trackers, check victory conditions, and prepare for the next round.
