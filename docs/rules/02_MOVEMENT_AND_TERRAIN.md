# Movement & Terrain

Bodacious Battles handles movement without the use of tape measures or free-form movement. Instead, the battlefield is broken up into predefined **Zones**.

## Moving Between Zones
During a unit's activation, it can spend Action Points (AP) to move from the Zone it currently occupies into an adjacent Zone. 
Because different Zones represent different types of terrain, the AP cost to enter a Zone varies:

- **Open Zone:** Costs **2 AP** to enter. (e.g., empty streets, flat fields).
- **Difficult Zone:** Costs **3 AP** to enter. (e.g., dense brush, steep stairs, rubble).
- **Hazardous Zone:** Costs **2 AP** to enter. However, when a unit enters this Zone, it immediately suffers 1 Wound (or must roll a specific hazard check if dictated by the scenario). (e.g., pools of acid, burning ground).

> [!TIP]
> **Example Movement:** A standard marine with 5 AP starts its activation. It spends 2 AP to move into an adjacent Open Zone, leaving it with 3 AP. It cannot move into a Difficult Zone (which requires 3 AP) because it wants to save 2 AP to make a Ranged Attack later in the turn.

## Cover Zones
Some Zones provide defensive benefits to the models occupying them. When a Zone is defined as Cover, it interacts directly with the **Defense Triad** during Ranged Combat. 

There are three types of Cover:
1. **Light Cover:** Grants models in this Zone **+1 Dodge** against Ranged attacks. (Ranged attacks hit them on a 5+ instead of a 4+). Represents fences, thin woods, or barricades.
2. **Heavy Cover:** Grants models in this Zone **+1 Dodge AND +1 Armor** against Ranged attacks. Represents bunkers, reinforced walls, or thick stone.
3. **Obscuring Cover:** Completely blocks Line of Sight. Ranged attacks cannot target models in an Obscuring Zone, nor can they trace a line of sight *through* an Obscuring Zone to hit something behind it. Represents smoke clouds or solid, windowless structures.

## Engaging & Falling Back
Models that occupy the same Zone are considered **Engaged**.
- While Engaged, models **cannot** make Ranged attacks. They can only make Melee attacks.
- If a model begins its activation in a Zone with enemy models, or moves into a Zone with enemy models, it is Engaged.
- **Falling Back:** Disengaging from a melee fight is difficult. If a model is Engaged and wishes to move to an adjacent Zone (leaving the enemy behind), it must pay **4 AP** instead of the normal movement cost.
