# Scenarios & Missions

*Draft - Subject to change.*

Games of Bodacious Battles are played using Scenarios to determine the winner. Pure deathmatch (just killing the opponent) often favors certain unit types over others. Scenarios force players to move, engage, and make tactical sacrifices.

## Scenario 1: Hold the Line (Zone Control)

**Setup:**
- Mark 3 Zones on the centerline of the battlefield as "Objective Zones". 
- Players deploy in the Zones along their respective board edges.

**Special Rules:**
- **Control:** At the end of every Game Round (after the bag is empty), players check the Objective Zones. A player *Controls* a Zone if they have more models physically inside that Zone than their opponent. Boss models count as 3 models for the purpose of controlling Zones.

**Scoring:**
- Score 1 Victory Point (VP) for each Objective Zone you control at the end of the round.
- The game lasts for 4 Rounds. The player with the most VP at the end wins.

## Scenario 2: Assassination

**Setup:**
- Players deploy in the Zones along their respective board edges.
- Before deployment, each player nominates one enemy model to be their **Primary Target**. If the enemy army consists of only one model (like a Boss), that model is automatically the target.

**Scoring:**
- Score 3 VP if you successfully destroy your Primary Target.
- Score 1 VP for every other enemy model destroyed (up to a maximum of 2 VP).
- The game ends immediately when a Primary Target is destroyed, or at the end of Round 4. The player with the most VP wins.
