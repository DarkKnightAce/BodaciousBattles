# The Command System

*Draft - Subject to change.*

Bodacious Battles features a force-wide resource called **Command Points (CP)**. This system allows players to unleash thematic abilities, alter the flow of combat, and react to unexpected situations.

## The Command Card & Tracking
Every player must bring a single **Command Card** for their army. This card lists their force's unique CP Generation mechanic and their Thematic Abilities. 

To track your current CP, simply place one or more D6s directly on your Command Card. (e.g., if you have 8 CP, you would place a D6 showing a '6' and a D6 showing a '2' on the card). As you spend or gain CP, adjust these dice accordingly. No custom tokens are required.

## Generating Command Points
At the start of the game, players receive a static amount of CP based on the agreed-upon game size (e.g., **10 CP** for a standard skirmish).

During the **Strategy Phase** of each round, players generate additional CP. However, this is not a flat rate. When building your army, you must select one **Command Generation Mechanic** from the list below that fits your force's theme and playstyle:

1. **Strategic Control:** Generate 1 CP for every Strategic Zone you currently control on the battlefield. (Best for defensive or objective-focused armies).
2. **Inspiring Leadership:** Generate 1 CP for every surviving unit in your army that has the **Leader** keyword. (Best for elite, character-driven armies).
3. **Bloodlust:** Generate 1 CP for every enemy unit that was destroyed during the previous round. (Best for highly aggressive, high-damage armies).
4. **Swarm Intelligence:** Generate 1 CP for every 2 friendly units currently on the battlefield. (Best for horde armies).

## Command Abilities
CP can be spent to activate powerful abilities. There are two categories:

### 1. Universal Abilities
These abilities are available to all players at all times, regardless of their faction:
- **Tactical Reroll [1 CP]:** Reroll any single die result. This can be used during your activation or when resolving enemy attacks against you.
- **Seize the Initiative [3 CP]:** Use this during the Action Phase instead of drawing from the bag. Immediately activate one of your eligible units. (You still remove one of your activation dice from the bag to balance the economy).

### 2. Thematic Abilities
These are powerful, force-specific abilities listed directly on your Command Card. When you design your custom army, you will select or create 2-4 Thematic Abilities. 

Below is a list of 12 thematic abilities spanning different genres that you can use or draw inspiration from:

#### Sci-Fi & Cybernetics
- **Phase Shift [2 CP]:** Select a friendly unit. Until the end of the round, it gains the **Teleport** keyword.
- **Nanite Reconstruction [3 CP]:** Select a friendly unit. Restore up to 3 lost Wounds to it (tracked by turning its Wound D6 up, not exceeding its maximum Wounds).
- **Overcharge Weapons [2 CP]:** Select a friendly unit. Until the end of the round, its Ranged attacks ignore 1 point of enemy Armor.
  > *Example: A high-tech soldier activates their plasma cell overdrive, burning through the armor of an approaching dreadnought.*

#### Fantasy & Sorcery
- **Arcane Shield [2 CP]:** Select a friendly unit. Until the end of the round, its Armor is increased by 1 (to a maximum of 4).
- **Necrotic Resurrection [5 CP]:** Select a destroyed friendly unit. Place it back on the battlefield in an Engaged Zone containing an enemy unit. It immediately makes a Melee attack.
- **Alchemical Mutagen [2 CP]:** Select a friendly unit. Until the end of the round, its CMB is increased by 2, but it suffers 1 Wound at the end of the round (cannot be reduced by Armor).

#### Modern & Weird War
- **Artillery Barrage [3 CP]:** Select a Zone. Until the end of the round, that Zone counts as a Hazardous Zone. Any unit entering that Zone immediately suffers 1 Wound (ignoring Armor).
- **Tactical Infiltration [3 CP]:** Select a friendly unit that has not activated yet. Immediately move it 1 Zone. This movement ignores enemy models in its starting Zone (does not cost Fall Back AP).
- **Smoke Screen [3 CP]:** Select an Open Zone. It counts as an Obscuring Cover Zone until the end of the round.

#### Post-Apocalyptic & Wasteland
- **Rad-Burst [3 CP]:** Select a Zone. Every unit in that Zone must roll a D6. On a 5+, that unit suffers 1 Wound (ignoring Armor).
- **Scavenge Scrap [1 CP]:** Use this ability immediately after an enemy unit is destroyed in a Zone containing a friendly model. You generate 2 CP.
  > *Example: Scavengers quickly strip a fallen enemy walker for battery packs, gaining a net profit of 1 CP.*

#### Gothic & Eldritch Horror
- **Grip of Terror [2 CP]:** Select an enemy unit in Line of Sight of a friendly unit. Until the end of the round, all actions (moving, attacking, etc.) performed by that unit cost 1 additional AP.

#### Steampunk & Clockwork
- **Steam Overdrive [3 CP]:** Select a friendly mechanical or vehicle unit. It immediately gains a free Melee Attack action (costs 0 AP), but it suffers 1 Wound at the end of its activation (cannot be reduced by Armor).
