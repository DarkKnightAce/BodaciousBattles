# Sample Units

These three sample units demonstrate how the formulaic point cost system works in practice. All units are tuned to easily fit into the standard 100-point game limit.

## 1. The Juggernaut (90 Points)
*A massive, heavily armored brute. Slow, but nearly impossible to kill with small arms fire. Due to its cost, it counts as a Boss and activates twice per round.*

**Base Profile Cost:** 5 pts
- **AP:** 4 (0 pts)
- **CMB:** 6 (+25 pts)
- **Wounds:** 6 (+25 pts)
- **Dodge:** 0 (0 pts)
- **Parry:** 0 (0 pts)
- **Armor:** 3 (+35 pts)

**Keywords:**
- **Boss** (Over 33 pts, 2 Activation Dice)

*Playstyle Note:* 90 points is almost your entire army. It will need to rely on its double activation and high armor to survive incoming fire while slowly closing the gap to crush enemies in melee.

---

## 2. Sniper Team (32 Points Each / 3 for 96 Points)
*Elite marksmen who strike from the shadows. Extremely hard to hit in return, but very frail if cornered.*

**Base Profile Cost:** 5 pts
- **AP:** 4 (0 pts)
- **CMB:** 4 (+15 pts)
- **Wounds:** 2 (+5 pts)
- **Dodge:** 2 (+20 pts)
- **Parry:** 0 (0 pts)
- **Armor:** 1 (0 pts)

**Keywords:**
- **Sniper (+10 pts):** Ignores Light Cover Dodge bonuses.
- **Clumsy / Frail (-10 pts):** Add +1 to incoming successful hits before Armor.

*Playstyle Note:* Keep these units in Cover to boost their Dodge to 3. Their frail nature means if any hits *do* get through, they will likely die instantly.

---

## 3. The Grunt Swarm (5 Points Each / 20 for 100 Points)
*A massive horde of poorly trained combatants. Their strength lies purely in numbers and overwhelming the enemy with sheer dice volume.*

**Base Profile Cost:** 5 pts
- **AP:** 4 (0 pts)
- **CMB:** 1 (0 pts)
- **Wounds:** 1 (0 pts)
- **Dodge:** 0 (0 pts)
- **Parry:** 0 (0 pts)
- **Armor:** 1 (0 pts)

**Keywords:** None.

*Playstyle Note:* Because they cost exactly the base 5 points, you can field 20 of these in a standard game. They will dominate scenario objective zones through sheer body count, but will die to almost any attack.
