# Sample Units

These sample units demonstrate how the formulaic point cost and Perk Point systems work in practice. All units are fully optimized to spend remaining Army Points and Perk Points where able, utilizing the complete list of modular keywords.

---

## 1. The Juggernaut (100 Points)
*A massive, heavily armored boss. Slow, but nearly impossible to kill with small arms fire. It activates twice per round and strikes with devastating force.*

**Stat Cost:** 100 Points
- **AP:** 4 (0 pts)
- **CMB:** 8 (+35 pts)
- **Wounds:** 6 (+25 pts)
- **Dodge:** 0 (0 pts)
- **Parry:** 0 (0 pts)
- **Armor:** 3 (+35 pts)
- **SPA:** 0 (0 pts)

**Perk Point Pool:** 100 Perk Points
- Free from Stat Cost (1:1): 100 Perk Points
- SPA Bonus: 0 Perk Points
- Negative Keywords: None

**Keywords Purchased (90 Perk Points Spent / 10 Left Unused):**
- **Boss (30 Perk Points):** Activates twice per round.
- **Teleport (15 Perk Points):** Once per game, spend 3 AP to blink to any zone in Line of Sight (represents high-speed thruster dashes).
- **Aura of Dread (15 Perk Points):** Enemies require +1 AP to Fall Back from this model's Zone.
- **Lethal Strike (10 Perk Points):** Natural 6s in melee ignore target's Parry and division by Armor.
- **Fortified (10 Perk Points):** Standing still grants +1 Armor against the next incoming attack.
- **Flying (10 Perk Points):** Ignores Difficult Terrain AP costs (represents crushing through obstacles).

*Playstyle Note:* At 100 points, this single model is your entire army. It uses Teleport to close the distance, Fortified to soak damage, and Lethal Strike to tear enemies apart in close combat.

## 2. Sniper Team (50 Points Each / 2 for 100 Points)
*Elite marksmen who strike from the shadows. Equipped with high-tech sniping braces and explosive ammunition, they infiltrate advanced positions early.*

**Stat Cost:** 50 Points
- **AP:** 4 (0 pts)
- **CMB:** 4 (+15 pts)
- **Wounds:** 2 (+5 pts)
- **Dodge:** 2 (+20 pts)
- **Parry:** 0 (0 pts)
- **Armor:** 1 (0 pts)
- **SPA:** 1 (+5 pts)

**Perk Point Pool:** 65 Perk Points
- Free from Stat Cost (1:1): 50 Perk Points
- SPA Bonus (+5 per level): 5 Perk Points
- Negative Keywords: **Clumsy / Frail** (+10 Perk Points)

**Keywords Purchased (55 Perk Points Spent / 10 Left Unused):**
- **Blast (15 Perk Points):** Ranged attacks deal an automatic hit to all other models in the target zone (explosive sniper rounds).
- **Ranged (10 Perk Points):** Equipped with modular sniper rifles.
- **Sniper (10 Perk Points):** Ignores Light Cover Dodge bonuses.
- **Vanguard (10 Perk Points):** Performs a free 1-Zone move before the first round starts.
- **Fortified (10 Perk Points):** Standing still grants +1 Armor (deploying heavy stabilizing tri-pods).

*Playstyle Note:* A standard army fields a pair of these marksmen. Use Vanguard to deploy them onto high ground or in Cover, then remain stationary to activate Fortified while bombarding enemy zones with explosive Blast fire.
